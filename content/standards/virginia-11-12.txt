Virginia Standards of Learning (SOL) — English — Grades 11-12
Source: doe.virginia.gov. Edit this file in admin to expand.

11.4 / 12.4  The student will read, comprehend, and analyze relationships among American literature, history, and culture (Grade 11) / Read, comprehend, and analyze the development of British literature and literature of other cultures (Grade 12).
  - Analyze the social or cultural function of American (or British / world) literature.
  - Examine a literary selection from several critical perspectives.
  - Recognize literary elements and figurative language and analyze their use.

11.5 / 12.5  The student will read and analyze a variety of nonfiction texts.

11.6 / 12.6  The student will write in a variety of forms, with an emphasis on persuasion (11) / informational, expository, and analytical writing (12).
  - Generate, gather, plan, and organize ideas for writing to address a specific audience and purpose.
  - Synthesize information from multiple sources to support a thesis.

11.7 / 12.7  The student will write, revise, and edit writing.
