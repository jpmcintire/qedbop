Virginia Standards of Learning (SOL) — English — Grades 9-10
Source: doe.virginia.gov. Edit this file in admin to expand.

9.4 / 10.4  The student will read, comprehend, and analyze a variety of fictional texts including poetry, narratives, and drama.
  - Identify literary and figurative devices and analyze their role in conveying meaning.
  - Analyze the relationship between form (sonnet, free verse, ballad, etc.) and meaning.
  - Make inferences and draw conclusions using textual evidence.
  - Analyze how an author's choices regarding structure, characterization, and language affect meaning.

9.5 / 10.5  The student will read, interpret, analyze, and evaluate a variety of nonfiction texts.

9.6 / 10.6  The student will develop a variety of writing to persuade, interpret, analyze, and evaluate with an emphasis on literary criticism.
  - Apply components of a recursive writing process for multiple purposes.
  - Cite evidence from texts to support claims.

9.1 / 10.1  The student will participate in, collaborate in, and make multimodal presentations both independently and in small groups.
